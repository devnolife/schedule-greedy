## ğŸ“ Logo Usage Guidelines

### âœ… Do's

1. **Use on appropriate backgrounds**
   - Primary logo: White or very light backgrounds (#FFFFFF, #F3F4F6)
   - Ensure high contrast for readability

2. **Maintain clear space**
   - Minimum clear space = height of "C" in ChronoSync
   - No other elements within this zone

3. **Use provided variations**
   - Large logo for headers
   - Compact logo for medium spaces
   - Minimal logo for small spaces

4. **Scale proportionally**
   - Always maintain aspect ratio
   - No distortion or stretching

5. **Use appropriate size**
   - Minimum width: 200px for primary logo

   - Maintain legibility

   - âŒ No 3D effects

4. **Don't rotate**
   - âŒ Logo should always be horizontal
   - âŒ No vertical or angled orientations
